### Features and thoughts
Let's Explore Job Opportunities. Jobbi assists you like a knowledgeable friend who is always updated on job openings. Start a conversation [here](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-rN9vFcix3-jobbi). Whether your interests lie in tech, design, or other fields, Jobbi is here to help.

Visit [http://jobbi.vercel.app/](http://jobbi.vercel.app/) to see what's available:
- **Assistant API**: More than a chatbot, Jobbi uses advanced AI to connect you with job opportunities that fit your skills and background.
- **Learning modules**: Enhance your qualifications with our tailored courses aimed at increasing your job prospects.
- **Job database**: Access a comprehensive collection of job listings from various sources in Norway, all consolidated in one location.

### NAV API Integration
**Service Description**
NAV offers a public API that provides access to job listings from NAV's database. These listings may include jobs posted directly to NAV as well as those sourced from NAV's partners. This integration allows Jobbi to offer users the most comprehensive and up-to-date job listings available in Norway.
**Who Can Use This Service**
The service is open to everyone and is free of charge, provided by NAV. It is designed to be accessible for any individual or organization interested in exploring a broad range of job opportunities. NAV reserves the right to revoke access in the case of service misuse.
