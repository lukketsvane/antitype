### Let's Talk Jobs

Chatting with Jobbi is like texting a friend who knows all about job openings. Try it out [here](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-rN9vFcix3-jobbi). Whether you're into tech, design, or anything in between, Jobbi's got your back.

### Dive into Jobbi's World

Ready for a deep dive? Head to [http://jobbi.vercel.app/](http://jobbi.vercel.app/) and explore:

- **Assistant API**: This isn't your average chatbot. It's a sophisticated AI that matches you with jobs based on your skills and experience.
- **Learning modules**: Sharpen your skills with on-site courses designed to boost your employability.
- **Job database**: A unified stream of job listings from various platforms in Norway, all in one place.
