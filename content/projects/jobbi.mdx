### Features and Thoughts
Jobbi serves as a resource for current job openings. Initiate a dialogue with Jobbi [here](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-rN9vFcix3-jobbi) to explore opportunities in tech, design, and other sectors.

Explore what Jobbi offers at [http://jobbi.vercel.app/](http://jobbi.vercel.app/):
- **Assistant API**: Utilizes AI to align you with job opportunities suited to your skills.
- **Learning modules**: Courses designed to enhance your career prospects.
- **Job database**: A comprehensive repository of job listings from various sources in Norway.

### NAV API Integration
**Service Description**
The NAV public API offers job listings from its database, encompassing both direct postings and those aggregated from partners. Jobbi accesses this API to provide up-to-date job listings throughout Norway.

**Who Can Use This Service**
The service is available for free to the public, offered by NAV. It is intended for anyone seeking a broad range of job opportunities. NAV maintains the authority to terminate access if the service is misused.
